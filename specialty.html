Beer for the people who like beer

Specialty beers, really anything that is great that doesn't fit in with the big boys. Including barleywines, porters, quads, old ale, fruit, spiced, experimental, and brown ales.

  • Barleywines are a strong ale typically having a high alcohol content range in colors from blonde to almost black.
    Quads are usually very high alcohol from the use of lots of malts.
    Old ale is similar in being a high abv beer from fermenting large amounts of the sugars in it.
    Porters are usually considered stouts little brother having typically a lower abv than a stout they are made using brown malt.
    While fruits and spices are used in a lot of beers, the ones without a distinct style background will go here as well.
    In addition anything barrel aged that isn't in Bourbon barrels or a stout base.
