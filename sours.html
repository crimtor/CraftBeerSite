Sours, like lemonade you pucker up for it

  • Sours are a newer addition to the beer scene and while the style has been made for at least a hundred years, at least the gose style it's just now becoming popular. The style is defined fairly simply as a beer with a sour taste, this can be caused by a couple of different things, but usually it's from a wild yeast or lacto.
  • There are several different sour beers but these are the most common;
    1. Gose
    2. Berliner Weisse
    3. Farmhouse
    4. Flanders
    5. or another style Ale with wild yeast added (especially Brett)
