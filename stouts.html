Stouts, the dark beer

  • Stouts are usually made with roasted malts or barley which impart a dark deep color on the beer. Flavors in them include: chocolate, toffee, milk, oatmeal, fruity, and spiced.
  • I like to seperate stouts into 3 categories;
    1. Bourbon Barrel Aged
    2. Strong
    3. and Sweet/Smooth
